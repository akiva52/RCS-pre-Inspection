# RCS Pre-Inspection Program — Setup Instructions
# Follow these steps exactly and you'll be live in about 30-45 minutes.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 1 — SET UP YOUR DATABASE IN SUPABASE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to https://supabase.com and log into your account
2. Open your "RCS Pre-Inspection" project
3. In the left sidebar, click "SQL Editor"
4. Click "New Query"
5. Open the file called SETUP_DATABASE.sql (included in this folder)
6. Copy the ENTIRE contents of that file
7. Paste it into the SQL Editor
8. Click the green "Run" button
9. You should see "Success" — your database is ready

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 2 — UPLOAD YOUR APP TO GITHUB
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to https://github.com and create a free account if you don't have one
2. Click the "+" button in the top right → "New repository"
3. Name it: rcs-pre-inspection
4. Keep it Private
5. Click "Create repository"
6. On the next screen, click "uploading an existing file"
7. Drag and drop ALL the files from this folder into the upload area
   (Make sure to upload the entire folder structure)
8. Click "Commit changes"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 3 — DEPLOY ON VERCEL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to https://vercel.com and log into your account
2. Click "Add New Project"
3. Click "Import Git Repository"
4. Connect your GitHub account if asked
5. Find "rcs-pre-inspection" and click "Import"
6. BEFORE clicking Deploy — click "Environment Variables"
7. Add these two variables:
   
   Name:  NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL
   Value: https://uneddweeletfgwzmfole.supabase.co

   Name:  NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY
   Value: sb_publishable_ns9hy0m62Fam5_qKkaRoEA_5w2OwfV_

8. Click "Deploy"
9. Wait about 2-3 minutes for it to build
10. Vercel will give you a URL like: https://rcs-pre-inspection.vercel.app

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 4 — TEST IT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open your Vercel URL on your phone
2. Tap "New Inspection" and set up a test facility
3. Add a few test issues
4. Open the same URL on your desktop — you should see everything synced
5. Try generating a report

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
YOU'RE LIVE!
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Bookmark the URL on your phone and desktop.
Add it to your phone's home screen for quick access:
  - iPhone: Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen"
  - Android: Chrome → Menu → "Add to Home Screen"

If anything doesn't work, come back to Claude and paste 
the error message — it will be fixed quickly.
